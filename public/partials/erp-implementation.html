ERP Implementation

Odoo is the world's easiest all-in-one management software. It includes hundreds of integrated business apps: CRM, Accounting, Inventory, Marketing, eCommerce, Project Management, HR, MRP, etc.

Odoo Services we offer

  • Packaged Installation :

    The package approach is an implementation mode that is low cost, generic, with few developments to be done. This approach shall be useful for the small and medium enterprises where most of the applications and report requirement are met by the Odoo Apps.

  • Custom Approach :

    This custom approach is an implementation mode which is used when the client’s business is weakly covered by Odoo generic. This approach goes from customer’s needs analysis, to Odoo solution.

  • Odoo Training :

    We provide training to your staff to make the most out of your odoo implementation.

  • Data Migration :

    We ensure your data is transfered to your new ODOO system in the easiest way possible.

Implementation Process

  1. Evaluate :- This is the pre-analysis study to understand the application, nature of business and the deployment target.
  2. Define the scope and Specifications:- This involves a detailed analysis and documentation of the entire business process. Broadly the scope of work and applications are defined. The level of customization if decided.
  3. Planning : The Planning for the deployment of the packing with respect to the given timeline is done.
  4. Configuration : - The system configuration and Development is undertaken.
  5. Data Migration : - The Data Migration through CSV or XML files is planned.
  6. Installation and Training : - The training is imparted to the users.
